The short version

I'm Jake Dart.

I help owner-operators stop being the bottleneck. I find the repetitive work that's eating your week, and I build it into systems that run on their own — for you, on your tools — then onboard you so you own every piece of it.

I didn't get here from a consulting deck. I got obsessed with using AI to run my own work, built an operating system to do it — voice tools, a knowledge spine, a library of one-click skills, dashboards that show me what's running — and realized that's the thing worth selling. Not advice. The system itself.

What I believe

  • You should own what you pay for. No lock-in, no black box — it lives on your accounts.
  • Never automate a broken process. Fix the workflow first, then hand the clean version to AI.
  • Price on value, not hours. If a build won't pay for itself several times over, I'll tell you not to do it.
  • Calm beats clever. The goal isn't more tools — it's a quieter, steadier business.
  • Build to completion. We're done when it runs without me, not when the call ends.
