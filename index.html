Questions

What people ask before we begin.

Why is the first call free?

Because you shouldn't pay anyone a dime before you know whether there's anything worth buying. In fifteen minutes you describe the task eating your week, and I name the one tool worth installing — with the real price — or tell you plainly that nothing is worth it yet. Either answer costs you nothing, and you can act on it with or without me.

Do I need to be technical?

No. Most of what I recommend is a tool that already exists — often something already sitting on your phone. You describe your worst hour of the week in plain English. That's the whole job. If a word like "API" ever comes out of my mouth, you're allowed to hang up.

What is a Company Brain?

A regular chatbot forgets you every conversation. A Company Brain is a private AI that actually knows your business — your files, your notes, your past decisions — so it can answer things like "what did we quote that customer last spring?" It runs on your own accounts, so your information stays yours, and for medical and dental offices it never holds patient records — business files only. If you ever stop paying me, you keep it. You're never renting back your own memory.

Do I have to keep going past the free call?

No. Most people stop wherever the value stops, and that's the point — nobody gets pushed past what's actually saving them hours. Each step builds on the last, but you decide how far to go, and every step is cancel-anytime.

Who is this for?

Small business owners and busy offices in Pocatello, Idaho Falls, Rexburg, and the surrounding area — shops, trades, studios, clinics, practices. If missed calls, confirmations, follow-up, or paperwork eat your week, that's the job. For medical and dental offices: no patient information ever touches anything I set up, and I'll tell you before you buy whether a tool will sign the paperwork your compliance requires.

How is this different from a big AI consultancy?

I live here. J&M is a small family business — me and my mom — and I show up in person when it makes sense. I name real prices out loud, and I'll tell you plainly when something isn't worth your money, including mine. No call center, no pitch deck, no contract to start.
